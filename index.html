Hello there!

I'm a PhD student in Computer Security at UCLouvain (ICTEAM), supervised by Pr. Etienne Rivière. I work on machine learning-based malware detection, with a focus on feature representations, evaluation methodology, and adversarial robustness. My work covers Windows PE malware and smart contract security.
